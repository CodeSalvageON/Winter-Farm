Welcome to the Style Guide!

Here, you'll find all you need to know in order to style your wiki.

Wiki page titles

Page titles are the first 50 characters before a title-marker.

Show Example 
<h1>A Fine Title</h1><p class="title-marker"></p>

Thus, the title of that page will be "A Fine Title".

Adding an info-box

Info-boxes denote people, events, and items.

Show Example 
<section class="info-box">
  <b><i>Duchy of Rome</i></b>
  <hr/>
  <img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/75/ByzantineEmpire717%2Bextrainfo%2Bthemes.svg" class="info-box-image"/>
  <hr/>
  <p>Info: Example</p>
  <p>More Info: Example</p>
</section>
